One of the seasonal attractions here is the rodeo at the end of July. many people here are very into rodeo sports and watching the show. Whenever someone visits, I suggest visiting the local parks if they have kids or driving and checking out trails and hikes in nearby parks/lakes. High Prairie has weekly markets where locals sell their products for us to enjoy.

Second Section

One of the main reasons people come to High Prairie is either one of two options. One they are returning to visit family and friends while on vacation. They are just passing through and get sucked in by the tiny town feel. When they see the community coming together when something devastating happens in the community, you can always tell that we care for everyone, despite what culture, religion, culture, or sexual orientation.
